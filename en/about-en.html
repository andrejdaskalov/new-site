About

Akva-Ing is a private company, formed 1995 in Stip, Republic of Macedonia. It was started in the area of hydrotechnics, design and engineering water supply systems, irrigation, treatment of waters, pools and fountains.

Services we offer:
-Architecture
-Construction
-Hydrotechnics -Environmental reports
-Supervision

In our list of finished projects we have a variety of clientelle, from individuals and private companies to municipalities and projects of state importance.
With the increase of experience, as well as the increase of our staff and client demand, we have increased our area of work to the level of the entire country
We expanded to architecture, designing houses, buildings, as well as major road infrastructure
Our employeess and partners are tried and tested in their respective fields and ready to take on any challenge the client can give. Our field of work is oriented towards consulting, design and supervision
